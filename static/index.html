Voice Bridge

User Guide

Welcome to Voice Bridge! This tool helps you seamlessly translate spoken audio between Hindi and Indonesian.

  1. Record your audio: Click the large microphone button to start recording your voice.
  2. Stop & Translate: Click the button again to stop. The app will immediately start translating your audio.
  3. Wait a moment: Our system will listen to the audio, translate the words, and generate a new voice speaking the translation.
  4. Review the results: You'll be able to listen to the new audio, read what was spoken, and see how fast the tool worked in the Dashboard below.
